About the Event

ABOUT 7 DAYS OF GLORY

COZA's "7 Days of Glory" is an annual event, a week-long period of fasting, prayer, and seeking God's direction, typically held at the beginning of July. It's a time for encountering God's presence and experiencing transformation. The event features powerful worship sessions, teachings from COZA's global senior pastors, Biodun and Modele Fatoyinbo, and is known for its life-changing impact

Frequently Asked Questions

Why 7 days of Glory?
It is a commanded fast.
I am in Abuja and would like to attend physically but have a transport issue?
There would be buses coming from every axis in Abuja that transports people to COZA Guzape Auditorium. Visit the PRU desk in any COZA Church location for more information.
How can I get the best out of 7DG?
Focus is very important during this time. Get messages, prayers, worship, a book and a pen. Write what God tells you. Write down prayers/thanksgiving points. It reminds you why you're fasting
What do I do when I feel tired while fasting?
Pray in tongues or switch to worship and your strength will be renewed
What am I to fast from?
You can fast off Habits, food, social media, TV, or any that takes your time
Will the Programs be stream?
Yes! The entire conference will be streamed for both morning and evening sessions.
