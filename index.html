About the Event

ABOUT 7 DAYS OF GLORY

7 Days of Glory is COZA's annual gathering of prayer, fasting and worship — seven set-apart days where we encounter God, receive His Word, and step into a new season of glory. Join thousands in Abuja and viewing centres around the world.

  • 7 days of prayer, the Word, and worship
  • Ministering from anointed servants of God
  • Powerful music ministrations every evening

Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What is 7 Days of Glory 2026?
7 Days of Glory (7DG) is COZA's annual seven-day gathering of prayer, fasting and worship. It's a season of encounter with God featuring anointed ministers, powerful music ministrations, and corporate prayer.
When and where will the event be held?
7DG 2026 holds from July 1–7, 2026 at the COZA Auditorium, Guzape Hills, Asokoro Extension, Abuja. Services run every morning at 6AM and evening at 6PM, with the Sunday service at 9AM.
How can I register to attend?
Registration is free. Simply use the Register button on this website to reserve your seat, indicate that you'll be joining online, or sign up to host a viewing centre.
Is there a fee to attend?
No. 7 Days of Glory is completely free and open to everyone, whether you join us in person in Abuja, online, or at a viewing centre near you.
Who can attend?
Everyone is welcome — members, first-time guests, and families. Come expectant for an encounter with God and an outpouring of His glory.
Can I join online?
Yes! Every service is streamed live on COZA TV and our social media platforms, so you can be part of all seven days from anywhere in the world. Replays are available after each session.
